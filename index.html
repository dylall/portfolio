How the pages differ

Portfolio page

Best when the goal is to show how you think, how you improve systems, and how you turn recurring friction into structured workflows.

Resume page

Best when the goal is to present career direction, role scope, and experience in a faster, more conventional format.

Combined use

Together, they separate proof of work from summary of career. One shows the machinery; the other shows the trajectory.
